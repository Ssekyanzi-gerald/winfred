About the Faculty of Science and Technology

The Faculty of Science and Technology at MUTEESA 1 ROYAL UNIVERSITY is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. We offer a wide range of programs in computer science, information technology, engineering, and applied sciences. Our state-of-the-art laboratories and experienced faculty provide students with the knowledge and practical skills needed to excel in the rapidly evolving fields of science and technology.

Our graduates are equipped to tackle global challenges and contribute to technological advancement in various industries. We foster a learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration.
